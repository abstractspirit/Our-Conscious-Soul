Fate of soulmates

“The pain is inevitable, suffering is optional” — an ancient thought. Our soul arrives on Earth in a specific place, within a specific life situation, and in a specific era in order to experience what it desires. We may not realize it, but in truth, we create our own lives — our inner spiritual reality creates these situations. What explains why some people become trapped in a negative spiral, repeatedly ending up in unhealthy relationships where they are abandoned, hurt, or made to feel unworthy? Their inner spiritual pattern continuously leads them there. They were made to believe that they are unlovable, and so their soul places them into situations where they can experience what they deeply believe to be true. Likewise, how is it that someone can find their true soulmate — someone with whom they can live a genuinely deep connection, a relationship not ruled by ego or circumstance, but by profound spiritual alignment and soulful connection? Diya Patel, a clairvoyant, perceives wandering souls, their desires, their suffering, and their connections with astonishing accuracy. From time to time, certain soul pairs become so strongly and deeply drawn to each other that they seem to weave through the entire Earth, and to clairvoyants they manifest as an intensely powerful energetic presence. According to Diya Patel, among the strongest current soul pairs is a Spanish woman and a Japanese man, who are almost exactly the same age. When they met, the woman immediately felt something unexplainable. The man, however, does not yet see things clearly on a spiritual level; he will only recognize and feel this connection years later. The second pair consists of a Canadian woman and an Austrian woman, who met through sports. Both experienced an unexplainable feeling toward the other. The Austrian woman is currently going through a great deal of suffering in her life, almost a form of atonement, as she punishes herself for her past poor decisions, while the Canadian woman is thriving — she has many friends and is making increasingly better choices that visibly elevate her life. The third pair is a Norwegian woman and a Hungarian man. There is an extraordinarily strong connection between their souls. They met during their university studies. At first, the man could not explain his feelings and was even afraid of them, as he is a highly pragmatic person who has never experienced anything like this and does not believe in spirituality. In a previous life, the two souls were lovers and deeply in love, which is the origin of the warm, familiar feeling he experiences toward a woman he barely knows. He tried to take steps toward her, but he is afraid of intense emotions and the unexplainable, so he remained very cautious and hesitant. This is a completely new situation for him, one he cannot confidently handle, even though he usually expects himself to manage every life situation with certainty. The woman, for a long time, did not fully feel or understand the connection, as she was deeply immersed in ego-driven concerns — outward appearance, performance, and meeting societal expectations. Only later did her soul awaken to the realization that she also feels something for him, and that she actually already knows his soul very well. The fourth pair is an Indian man and an Egyptian woman. Their connection is significantly complicated by religion and life circumstances, yet a clearly visible force pulls them toward each other. The Indian man is struggling with many internal issues and is trying to escape an extremely difficult life situation, while the woman is emotionally very wounded, and this connection represents a form of escape for her. They met while the man was working abroad and the woman was traveling, and both felt quite clearly that this was a deeper form of connection. At present, these four are considered the strongest soul pairs. Diya Patel and other clairvoyants are deeply fascinated by their connections and are eagerly watching how their stories will unfold.
